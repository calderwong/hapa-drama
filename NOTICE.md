# Notices

`hapa-drama` is a Hapa voice synthesis node scaffold.

Optional upstream engines are not vendored by default:

- DramaBox: https://github.com/resemble-ai/DramaBox
- MLX-Audio: https://github.com/Blaizzy/mlx-audio
- Piper TTS: https://github.com/rhasspy/piper

Generated audio, runtime databases, model weights, tokens, and local artifacts are runtime data and must stay out of git.
